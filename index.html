Basic Info About Me

Hey there, I'm Asmin, a 22-year-old guy from the picturesque town of Rangeli. Currently, I'm deep into my 3rd semester at BMC, Dudhpati, where I'm immersing myself in the world of coding. While I may consider myself a beginner, my passion for coding is anything but novice. It's my digital playground, where I relish the thrill of experimenting and the joy of acquiring new skills.

Speaking of challenges, I've set my sights on something big: exploring the heart of the tech universe, Silicon Valley. The boundless innovation and opportunities that thrive there have me captivated. I'm dedicated to honing my skills and knowledge, with the dream of one day being an integral part of that tech wonderland.

And then there's my ultimate goal, which is to make my mark in America, possibly securing permanent residency. It's a monumental undertaking, but I'm all in.

Join me on my journey as I transform my coding dreams into a tangible reality. If you share my enthusiasm for tech and coding, I'd love to connect. Feel free to reach out for a chat or with any questions. Together, we can dive into the captivating world of coding, share knowledge, and transform our dreams into achievements. Let's embark on this exciting journey of growth and discovery!
